About Me

Since graduating in 2015 with a degree in Music Business, I moved to New York City and spent the last 5 years bartending and working event production while playing guitar in multiple bands. After pandemic hit, I turned to learning programming as a way to stay intellectually stimulated and challenged during lockdown. Since then, I have built personal projects with multiple different languages, contributed to existing projects and have started taking on freelance clients as well.

In my free time I enjoy playing and writing music, going to shows, exercising and eating good food.

If you have a project you would like to discuss, I would love to hear from you!
